Featured Destinations in Bihar

Mahabodhi Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Bodh Gaya, marking the place where Buddha attained enlightenment. A major pilgrimage site for Buddhists worldwide.

Nalanda University Ruins

Explore the remains of the ancient Mahavihara, a large Buddhist monastery and a renowned center of learning in ancient Magadha.

View on Map

Rajgir

An ancient city and popular tourist spot known for its hot springs, Vishwa Shanti Stupa accessed by a ropeway, and historical sites related to Buddhism and Jainism.

View on Map

Takht Sri Patna Sahib

The birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh Ji, the tenth Sikh Guru. A sacred pilgrimage site for Sikhs, located in Patna.

View on Map

Vikramshila Ruins

Ruins of another ancient Buddhist university, located near Bhagalpur. Offers insights into the educational and religious practices of the past.

View on Map

Mahavir Mandir, Patna

Founded by Swami Balanand in 1730 A.D., Mahavir Mandir in Patna gained popularity after the 1947 partition due to the influx of refugees. Originally rebuilt as a concrete structure then, it was transformed into a large marble temple in 1987. The temple is renowned for housing the idol of Sankat-Mochan, an avatar of Hanuman.
